Tiago de Freitas Pereira

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Idiap Research Institute, where I work in the Biometrics group.

I'm interested in face and speaker recognition, presentation attack detection in biometric systems (aka antispoofing), computer vision, machine learning and optimization.

I did my PhD at École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), where I was advised by Sébastien Marcel. I've carried out my masters at University of Campinas (UNICAMP) and did my bachelor at the University of São Paulo ( USP ).
